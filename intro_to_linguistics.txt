* Syntax -- the structure of language, such as English being a an svo language
* a tree can be contructed for representation of sentences
* Semantics --  the meaning of language, including the ambiguity of language
* interpretation is based on context
* Morphology -- structure of words, with a morphene being the smallest unit of meaning
               ex: when the "ment" suffix is added to the verb "establish" is becomes a noun
                   making ment a derivational morphene
* Phonetics -- properties of sounds
               ex: the difference between an |s| sound and |z| sound is simply the vibration
                   of vocal folds
* Phonolgy -- paterns of sounds
              ex: pay, bay, ebay
